# Chapter 6: Exam Tips  
### Chinese Arbitration Law – Academic Guide

---

## 1. Introduction

Exam questions often test arbitration clause validity and jurisdiction analysis.

---

## 2. 5-Step Method

1. Identify arbitration clause  
2. Check validity  
3. Identify institution  
4. Determine jurisdiction  
5. Write conclusion  

---

## 3. Validity Checklist

- Written agreement  
- Clear intention  
- Defined scope  
- Identifiable institution  

---

## 4. Common Mistakes

- Ignoring validity  
- Missing institution check  
- No structured answer  

---

## 5. Conclusion

A structured answer is more important than length in exams.
