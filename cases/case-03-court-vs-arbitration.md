# Case 03: Court vs Arbitration Conflict

## Facts

Company A and Company B include this clause:

> “All disputes shall be submitted to CIETAC for arbitration.”

Despite this, Company A files a lawsuit in court.

## Issue

Should the court accept jurisdiction?

## Analysis

There is a valid arbitration agreement because:

* The intention to arbitrate is clear
* The arbitration institution (CIETAC) is identified
* The scope covers all disputes

Under Chinese arbitration principles, courts generally must respect valid arbitration agreements.

## Conclusion

The court should decline jurisdiction and refer the parties to arbitration.

## Exam Tip

A valid arbitration clause usually excludes court jurisdiction unless the clause is invalid or non-arbitrable.
