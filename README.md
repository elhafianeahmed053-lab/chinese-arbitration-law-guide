# Chinese Arbitration Law for International Students

A practical guide to Chinese arbitration law for international students, researchers, and arbitration competition participants.

## Topics Covered

- Arbitration Agreements
- Jurisdiction Rules
- Validity of Arbitration Clauses
- Case Studies
- Exam Tips
- References

## Repository Structure

- docs/
- cases/
- diagrams/
- exam-materials/

## Purpose

This repository aims to provide clear and accessible explanations of Chinese arbitration law and help students prepare for exams, moot courts, and legal research.
