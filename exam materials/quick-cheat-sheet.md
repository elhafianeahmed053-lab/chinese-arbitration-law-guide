# Quick Cheat Sheet

## Valid Arbitration Clause Checklist

✓ Written agreement
✓ Clear intention to arbitrate
✓ Scope of disputes defined
✓ Arbitration institution identified

---

## Jurisdiction Rule

* Valid arbitration agreement → arbitration tribunal has jurisdiction
* Invalid agreement → court has jurisdiction

---

## Common Invalid Clauses

❌ Arbitration OR litigation option
❌ No arbitration institution
❌ Unclear intention

---

## Exam Method (5 Steps)

1. Identify clause
2. Check validity
3. Identify institution
4. Determine jurisdiction
5. Conclude clearly

---

## Key Idea

👉 Chinese courts generally respect valid arbitration agreements and will not interfere.
