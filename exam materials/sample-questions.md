# Sample Exam Questions

## Question 1

A contract contains the clause:

> “Any dispute arising from this contract shall be submitted to CIETAC for arbitration.”

Is this clause valid under Chinese arbitration law?

---

## Question 2

A clause states:

> “Disputes may be resolved by arbitration or litigation.”

Discuss the validity of this clause.

---

## Question 3

A contract contains an arbitration clause, but no arbitration institution is specified.

What is the legal effect of this clause?

---

## Question 4

If a party files a lawsuit in court despite a valid arbitration agreement, how should the court respond?

---

## Question 5

Explain the requirements for a valid arbitration agreement under Chinese law.
